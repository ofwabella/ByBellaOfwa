The making of
By Bella Ofwa

Adaptive Construction. Timeless Restraint.

Bella Ofwa Founder & Designer

"I've always been a maker."

From sewing by hand as a child, to teaching myself to crochet during 2020, I've always been drawn to creating with my hands. At some point I bought a sewing machine with no idea how to use it - then went straight to YouTube University, Pinterest Library, and TikTok Tuition.

I started taking small orders from family and friends, and eventually launched a bedroom-based brand. But I hit a realization: I didn't fully understand how garments actually adapt to different bodies. And that mattered more than I thought.

So I paused. Went back to school, and took up Pattern Drafting & Grading Training at NITA - which completely shifted how I see clothing.

I've also studied Architecture - and it taught me to design from whole to part, to break things into systems, and to believe that almost any form is possible. You just have to figure out how to construct it.
